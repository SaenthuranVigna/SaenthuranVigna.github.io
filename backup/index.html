Hey! Welcome to my Page :)

I'm guessing you're here because you have some sort of idea/dream you want to explore. Well, you've come to the right place. Let me give you some context so you understand a little more about what I do.

Passionate About Learning

I'm super passionate about learning, and my whole goal is to make learning more accessible for everyone. I believe everyone should have something they're passionate about and can learn. My mission is to help you make those ideas and dreams a reality, for FREE.

What's the Catch?

The way I see it, the world needs more dreamers and passionate people doing what they love. If money is the only thing standing in the way of someone chasing their dream, I'm all in to help remove that barrier. Sometimes, all it takes is finding the right opportunities, learning a bit, and having the passion to go for it.

Your Journey from Idea to Build

My role in your journey is to equip you with the resources and knowledge you need to take that first step and learn the right things. After you bring your idea to life, there are two options:

  1. I can drop off at that point, and you can continue building your idea without me.
  2. We can continue working together and set up a paid arrangement where I still provide value through coaching calls.

Whichever option you choose, all I ask is that you keep in touch. If everything goes according to plan, you'll be vastly successful, and I'd love to watch you grow and make your dreams a reality.

The Art of Coffee Chatting

"The Art of Coffee Chatting" is a series of virtual coffee calls with interesting people who have had unique journeys or are building something cool. If you want to be on the show and you fall into one of the following categories, message me:

  • You're building something cool
  • You took a non-traditional route to get to where you are professionally
  • You know someone who fits the above criteria that you think I should meet

Digital Resources

I create digital resources like eBooks and templates so you can learn at least a little about the steps you need to take more generally. If you want a specific resource, template, or eBook, and it falls into one of the following categories, message me, and I'll make it just for you:

  • It has to be something general (valuable to a slightly larger audience)
  • It must not be time-sensitive (needs to be something that I can spend a reasonable amount of time on, so it's actually valuable for you)
